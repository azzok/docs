Your Privacy Matters

This policy explains what information Luna TicTacToe collects, how it's used, and your rights as a player. Please read it carefully.

1 Introduction

Welcome to Luna TicTacToe ("we", "our", or "us"). This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and share information when you use our mobile application Luna TicTacToe (the "App"), available on Android and iOS.

By downloading or using our App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

2 Information We Collect

Account Information

When you sign in using Google, Apple, or Email, we collect your name, email address, and profile picture provided by those services. Guest accounts are created with an anonymous identifier — no personal information is required.

Game Data

We store your game history, match results (wins/losses), score, in-game coins, and selected skins to provide a personalized experience across sessions.

Device & Usage Information

We may collect device type, operating system version, app version, crash reports, and usage analytics to improve app performance and stability.

Purchase Information

If you make an in-app purchase, the transaction is processed by Google Play or the Apple App Store. We do not collect or store your payment card details. We only receive confirmation of the purchase to unlock the relevant content.

3 How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your player account and game profile
  • To enable online multiplayer, match history, and leaderboards
  • To process and verify in-app purchases and unlock purchased content
  • To display relevant advertisements through Google AdMob and Unity Ads
  • To monitor app performance, diagnose crashes, and fix bugs
  • To comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Service

4 Third-Party Services

Our App uses the following third-party services. Each has its own privacy policy governing the use of your information.

Firebase (Google)

Authentication, Realtime Database, Storage, Analytics, and App Check.

Google Sign-In

Used to authenticate players via their Google account.

Sign in with Apple

Used to authenticate players via their Apple ID on iOS devices.

Google AdMob

Serves ads within the App and may collect advertising identifiers.

Unity Ads

Serves video ads within the App and may collect device information.

Google Play Billing / App Store

Handles payment processing for in-app purchases.

5 Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely on Google Firebase servers. Firebase uses industry-standard encryption in transit (TLS) and at rest. We use Firebase App Check to ensure only legitimate versions of our App can access our backend services.

While we take reasonable steps to protect your information, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

6 Children's Privacy

Luna TicTacToe is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

7 Your Rights

  • Correction — request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion — request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Opt-out of ads — adjust your device's ad personalization settings at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at lunawhizz@gmail.com.
